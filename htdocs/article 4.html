Two filipina teens qualify to become part of rising kpop group UNIS.

Two more Filipina trainees are set to debut in the K-pop girl group “UNIS,” after securing their respective debut spots on the South Korean survival show “Universe Ticket.”These trainees are Gehlee Dangca and Filipino-Korean Jin Hyeon-ju. In the finale of the said show that aired on January 17, Gehlee secured his spot as the 4th member after he advanced to P-level, the highest ranking in the survival show. These two teens join elisia, their fellow filipina in the group. Making the three join the other 4 Filipina Kpop idols. Totalling to 7 Filipina Kpop idols in the world. This marks a historic event in the Kpop industry showing more representation to various countries. Leading the way in the music wave.
